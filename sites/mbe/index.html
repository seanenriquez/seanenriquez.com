Burgers

All of our burgers are made from fresh, local ingredients, ensuring the best possible bang for your buck! We only use seasonal ingredients and work with local farmers to insure that our promise of quality is kept. 100% grass fed humanely treated local cows are the only option for us! We believe a happy cow makes for a happy customer!

All burgers are made to order* and come with a side of fries or fruit cup. Side salad is a 1.99 extra.

Hotdogs

Like our burgers, we only use local fresh ingredients when preparing our famous hotdogs. We do all grinding and casing of our hotdogs in house to ensure a quality of beef that isn't composed of 'other' ingredients. Our buns are made fresh every day along with our inhouse condiments including our Sweet Sunset mustard and Savory Kickin' relish.
